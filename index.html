The U.S. Constitution defines the structure of the Government. But unlike most
(any?) other constitutions, it also represents a code of ethics, similar to the
Boy Scout Oath, the Oath of Hippocrates, and the Ten Commandments.

If you are unwilling or unable to read, understand, and abide by those ethics,
you have no business working in the U.S. Government, at any level.

- D.W.Langford
